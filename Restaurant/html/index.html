About Us
Mister Cook, established in 2006 in the heart of Souss capital, is the first fast-food chain in Agadir. It is a story of a brand that grows and achieves tremendous success over the years! The secret to our remarkable growth lies in our exceptional expertise, lauded unanimously by our valued customers who vouch for the quality of our products and services. We meticulously choose our raw materials from trusted suppliers, emphasizing quality. Additionally, we pay special attention to the training of our staff, who are the flag bearers of our savoir-faire. Moreover, Mister Cook is constantly on the lookout for innovation and creativity to offer our loyal customers a wide range of high-quality products. Customer satisfaction is our top priority.
